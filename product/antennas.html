    Dish antenna are commonly used for high gain directive data communications such as wireless WAN/LAN links. A pair of dish antenna creates a high-performance, point-to-point backhaul as shown below The innovative mechanical design allows AP/Bridge circuit board inside the feed antenna.



    Panel antenna is a directional antenna typically with narrower transmission beam than sector antenna. It is designed to improve RF performance in long range point-to-point deployments. Senao Networks designs a variety of mounting brackets pole mount or wall mount with angle adjustment for different applications. We also work with system integrator to attach panel antenna directly to AP to simplify installation and save operating cost.




    Sector antenna restricts emitted energy to a sub-circular arc and narrow vertical coverage. It makes efficient use of transmission power. To increase or widen coverage area, several sector antennas are installed on the same supporting structure pointing to different angles.
    Senao Sector antennas are in robust mechanical design for outdoor application use. The sector antenna series features a heavy-duty plastic for all-weather operation. The mounting system adjusts from 0 to 10 degrees down-tilt, ideal for wireless Internet "Cell" Sites.

    Key Features

    Enterprise-class, long-range, 120°2x2, 3x3 and 4x4MIMO

    Plug-in mounting system compatible for APs with external connectors

    Coverage up to 3km (1.86miles)

    Adjustable down-tilt ranges from 0 to10°

    Compact IP55-rated enclosure

    Resists harsh conditions of wing gusts to 125mph


    Dipole Antenna


    Senao Networks’ 2.4 GHz and 5GHz N-plug dipole antennas provide excellent gain and full 360° coverage beamwidth, making it ideal for outdoor wireless communications.


    Antenna Array & PIFA Module


    The increasingly popular compact design of AP requires its antenna to be blended into overall industrial design in a modularized form factor. Planar Inverted-F antenna (PIFA) is the most popular structure for internal antenna module widely used in compact hand-held devices to provide various wireless transmissions such as GSM, Bluetooth, LTE and WiFi.
